About ADAX Nominated Advisors Program
The role of the ADAX Nominated Advisor ("Nomad‚Äù) is to advise, guide and train companies willing to list their asset-backed tokens on the ADAX platform. It is also a pledge to fulfill ongoing commitments upon listing.

A NOMAD is an advisory company approved by the ADAX compliance team and generally represents one of the following industries:
  • Investment Bank/brokerage House
  • Corporate Finance
  • Legal Advisory
  • Accounting
  • Outsourcing Services Providers
The ADAX compliance team will conduct a detailed background search to verify and approve each NOMAD.Applicants must provide a minimum of one consultant who become the liaison with ADAX and the NOMAD company by passing a screening test with a minimum score of 95%.

The applicant becomes a Nomad when:
  • Approved by ADAX Compliance Team
  • Signs a Nomad Agreement With ADAX
  • Shows proof for the required minimum staking amount of HYB tokens in their ADAX wallet
Following the completion of these requirements, ADAX issues a partnership certificate relative to the corresponding level of participation.
